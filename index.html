Change

No matter how the elections turn out this year, there is one sure thing: the next president will a change unlike nothing the country has seen before. The country is witnessing candidates who would have been thought unthinkable in the past and old conventions are making way for new ones. There are many candidates and a clear winner is not in sight. Donald Trump, Hillary Clinton, Bernie Sanders and to a much lesser extent John Kassich all remain in the running.
